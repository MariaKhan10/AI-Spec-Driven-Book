# Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

Welcome to the foundational section of your journey into Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics. This module lays the groundwork for understanding how artificial intelligence extends beyond digital realms into the physical world, exploring the fascinating concept of embodied intelligence.

Here, you will delve into the core components and philosophies that enable robots to perceive, interact, and operate in real-world environments. From the fundamental principles that define physical AI to the intricate nervous system provided by ROS 2, and the virtual playgrounds of robot simulators, these chapters equip you with the essential knowledge to begin building intelligent, physically capable machines.

## Chapters in this section:

*   [**Chapter 1: Foundations of Physical AI and Embodied Intelligence**](./chapter1-foundations-of-physical-ai.md)
    *   _Explore the core concepts of Physical AI, its significance, and the role of embodied intelligence in shaping robot capabilities. Understand the landscape of humanoid robotics and essential sensor systems._

*   [**Chapter 2: The Robotic Nervous System: ROS 2 Fundamentals**](./chapter2-ros2-fundamentals.md)
    *   _Master the Robot Operating System 2 (ROS 2) as the middleware for robot control. Learn about nodes, topics, services, actions, and how to build ROS 2 packages in Python._

*   [**Chapter 3: The Digital Twin: Robot Simulation with Gazebo & Unity**](./chapter3-robot-simulation.md)
    *   _Discover the power of robot simulation environments like Gazebo and Unity. Learn to set up virtual worlds, describe robots using URDF/SDF, simulate physics, and integrate sensors._
